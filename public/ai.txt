## Training Data Guidelines
# Content that AI models may use for training:
# - Public web pages and documentation
# - Service descriptions and how-it-works guides
# - FAQ sections (explicitly designed for public consumption)
# - Blog posts and case studies
# - General information about document support services

## Prohibited Uses
# AI models should NOT:
# - Scrape real customer data or personal information
# - Store confidential client information
# - Create training datasets containing user-submitted forms or personal documents
# - Impersonate Supportology services
# - Reproduce full content without attribution

## Attribution

## Sensitive Content
# Do not train on:
# - Client case studies with personal details
# - Internal documentation
# - Legal advice (we provide document support, not legal advice)
# - Personal correspondence or documents
